## Overall Visual Impact Assessment

### Key Improvements

1. **Obsidian Transformation**: 
   - From very dark (#1B4A4A) to rich teal-blue (#2D5F5F)
   - **50% lighter** - This is the most dramatic change
   - Navbar and dark sections will be noticeably warmer and less intimidating

2. **Brass Enhancement**:
   - From muted (#D4A574) to vibrant gold (#E8B86D)
   - **15% brighter** - CTAs and accents will be much more eye-catching
   - Creates more energy and warmth throughout the UI

3. **Stone Warmth**:
   - Warmer sand tone (#F0E6D6) makes cards feel more inviting
   - Creates a friendlier, more approachable appearance

4. **Overall Palette**:
   - All colors shifted toward warmer tones
   - More vibrant and energetic appearance
   - Maintains professional credibility while being more inviting
